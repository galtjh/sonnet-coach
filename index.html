Meter reminder

Aim for iambic pentameter, but treat substitutions as expressive tools. A trochee can add punch. A spondee can slow the air.

Workshop rule

Don’t solve the poem too neatly. Strong sonnets transform, complicate, or surprise instead of summarizing.
